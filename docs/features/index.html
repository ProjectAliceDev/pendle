About AliceOS

Meet your new Ren'Py framework.

Get Started

Fast install, faster upgrades.

AliceOS is easy to install with just adding a single file to your existing Ren'Py visual novel project. And with each new release, all you have to do is just replace that file. AliceOS also builds with the latest Ren'Py SDKs and special editions for projects like mods for Doki Doki Literature Club.

Super extensible with apps.

AliceOS adds great new tools and utilities to let you take advantage of what AliceOS has to offer. AliceOS comes bundled with applications like App Manager and inventories, and developers can make use of AppKit and ScreenKit to create custom apps for AliceOS with ease.

You're in control. Always.

Privacy and security matter. That's why we bake it directly into AliceOS with AppKit. Apps only have access to the things you want them to with your permission. For developers, it's just as easy to ask and work with permissions.

Super customizable. Down to the source.

AliceOS offers easy modularity with frameworks that can be added, modified, or removed in the AliceOS package. And since AliceOS is open-source under the BSD-2-Clause license, anyone can create custom AliceOS packages easily.
